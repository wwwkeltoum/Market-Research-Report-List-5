<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Alloy Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crystal-Based Piezoelectric Alloy Powder</li><li>Ceramic-Based Piezoelectric Alloy Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric alloy powder market is primarily divided into two types: crystal-based and ceramic-based piezoelectric alloy powders. Crystal-based piezoelectric powders, typically composed of materials like quartz or tourmaline, offer high sensitivity and are ideal for precision applications. In contrast, ceramic-based piezoelectric powders, often made from materials like lead zirconate titanate (PZT), are favored for their durability and versatility, making them suitable for a broader range of industrial applications. Both types play critical roles in sensors, actuators, and energy harvesting devices.</p></p>
