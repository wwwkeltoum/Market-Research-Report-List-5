<p><p>The short-range wireless communication market utilizes various inductor types, each designed for specific applications. Multilayer type inductors consist of multiple stacked layers, providing high inductance in a small footprint, making them ideal for mobile devices. Film type inductors utilize thin films for low inductance values and superior performance in high-frequency circuits. Each type plays a critical role in optimizing signal integrity and minimizing interference in wireless communication systems.</p></p>
