<p><ul><li>Contact Roughness and Contour Measuring Machine</li><li>Non-Contact Roughness and Contour Measuring Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roughness and contour measuring machine market is segmented into two main types: contact and non-contact measuring machines. Contact roughness and contour measuring machines utilize probes that physically make contact with the surface to measure texture and profile, providing high precision. In contrast, non-contact measuring machines employ optical methods like laser or white light to assess surface characteristics without touching the material, making them ideal for delicate or soft surfaces. Both types serve essential roles in quality control across various industries.</p></p>
