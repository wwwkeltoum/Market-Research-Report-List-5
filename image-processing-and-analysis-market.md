<p><p>The image processing and analysis market encompasses various techniques that enhance or analyze visual data. Point processing involves manipulating individual pixels, focusing on adjustments like brightness and contrast. Group processing looks at clusters of pixels to extract features or apply filters for noise reduction. Geometry processing deals with the shape and structure of image elements, enabling tasks like segmentation and edge detection. Frame processing analyzes video frames to improve motion detection and video quality, catering to real-time applications.</p></p>
