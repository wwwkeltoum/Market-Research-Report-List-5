<p><strong>The Vertical Axis Wind Turbine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Darrieus</li><li>Savonius</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vertical axis wind turbine (VAWT) market is primarily divided into two types: Darrieus and Savonius turbines. Darrieus turbines feature a curved blade design that efficiently captures wind from any direction, making them suitable for urban environments with turbulent airflow. In contrast, Savonius turbines have a scoop-like design ideal for low wind speed areas, offering simplicity and ease of maintenance. Both turbine types serve specific applications, catering to diverse energy needs and contributing to the growing renewable energy landscape.</p></p>
