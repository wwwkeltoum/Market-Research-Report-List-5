<p><p>Floor-standing 3D printers are large-scale devices designed for industrial or professional use. The FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) market focuses on printers that extrude thermoplastic filaments layer by layer, offering cost-effective solutions for prototyping and functional parts. The SLA (Stereolithography) market involves printers using UV lasers to cure liquid resin into precise, high-detail models, ideal for applications requiring fine resolution, such as dental or jewelry industries. Both markets cater to diverse industries, with FDM emphasizing affordability and durability, while SLA prioritizes precision and surface finish.</p></p>
