De-inked pulp is recycled paper that has undergone a cleaning process, making it environmentally friendly while maintaining good quality for various applications. Mixed pulp combines both virgin and recycled materials, offering a balance between cost-efficiency and performance, catering to diverse consumer needs in the packaging industry.</p></p>
