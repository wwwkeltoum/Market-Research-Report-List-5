<p><p>The water-based polyurethane dispersion (PUD) market is divided into three main types: polyester, polyether, and polycarbonate. Polyester-based PUDs offer excellent mechanical properties and chemical resistance, making them suitable for coatings and adhesives. Polyether-based PUDs provide superior flexibility, water resistance, and low-temperature performance, ideal for applications in textiles and foams. Polycarbonate-based PUDs are known for their outstanding thermal stability and durability, often used in high-performance coatings and industrial applications. Each type caters to specific industry demands.</p></p>
