<p><p>The concrete repair product market is primarily segmented into one-component and two-component mortars. One-component mortars are ready-to-use, requiring only water for activation, making them convenient for quick repairs. In contrast, two-component mortars consist of a base and a hardener that must be mixed before application, offering enhanced performance and durability for demanding repairs. Both types cater to varying project needs, from simple patching to extensive structural restorations, underlining their significance in construction and maintenance.</p></p>
