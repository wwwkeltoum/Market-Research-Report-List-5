<p><ul><li>Single-Row</li><li>Double-Row</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roller bearings market is categorized into single-row and double-row types. Single-row roller bearings are designed to accommodate radial loads effectively and are compact, making them ideal for applications with limited space. In contrast, double-row roller bearings feature two rows of rolling elements, providing increased load capacity and enhanced stability, suitable for higher loads and applications with significant axial stress. These types cater to various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, ensuring efficient performance and reliability.</p></p>
