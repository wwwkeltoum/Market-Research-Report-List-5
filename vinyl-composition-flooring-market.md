<p><p>Vinyl Composition Flooring (VCF) market is segmented into two main types: single-layer and multilayer. Single-layer VCF consists of a single sheet of vinyl, offering a cost-effective and straightforward solution, suitable for low-traffic areas. In contrast, multilayer VCF features multiple layers that enhance durability, resilience, and design versatility, making it ideal for high-traffic commercial spaces. The multilayer option typically includes a robust backing, decorative layer, and a protective wear layer, providing superior performance and aesthetic appeal for various applications.</p></p>
