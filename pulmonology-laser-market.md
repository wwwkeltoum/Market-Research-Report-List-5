<p><strong>The Pulmonology Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>YAG Laser</li><li>Photodynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pulmonology laser market features two primary types: YAG lasers and photodynamic therapy (PDT). YAG lasers, specifically neodymium-doped yttrium aluminum garnet lasers, are used for precise surgical procedures, such as tumor removal in the lungs, due to their ability to penetrate tissue effectively. In contrast, photodynamic therapy utilizes photosensitive drugs activated by specific light wavelengths to target and destroy cancerous cells within the lungs. Both technologies enhance treatment options for respiratory conditions, improving patient outcomes and advancing lung health care.</p></p>
