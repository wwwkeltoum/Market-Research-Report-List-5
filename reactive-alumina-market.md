<p><p>The Reactive Alumina market is divided into two primary types: Powdered Form Reactive Alumina and Sphered Form Reactive Alumina. Powdered Form is characterized by fine particles that offer high surface area, ideal for applications in catalysts and adsorbents. Sphered Form, on the other hand, features uniform spherical granules that enhance flowability and packing density, making it suitable for uses like ceramic production and advanced filtration systems. Both forms cater to diverse industrial needs, leveraging their specific properties for optimized performance.</p></p>
