<p><ul><li>Convection Ovens</li><li>Vapour Phase Ovens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surface Mount Technology (SMT) reflow oven market encompasses various types, primarily convection ovens and vapor phase ovens. Convection ovens utilize forced airflow to evenly distribute heat, ensuring consistent soldering across components. They are widely favored for their efficiency and flexibility in handling various board sizes. In contrast, vapor phase ovens operate by immersing the assembly in a vaporized solvent that condenses on cooler surfaces, providing precise temperature control and preventing thermal stresses. Each type serves different production needs and quality requirements in electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
