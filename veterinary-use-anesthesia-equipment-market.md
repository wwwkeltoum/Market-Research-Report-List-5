Ventilators support breathing in animals under sedation, ensuring adequate oxygen supply. Vaporizers mix anesthetic gases with carrier gases for precise delivery. Waste gas management systems safely remove and neutralize excess anesthetic gases, protecting staff and patients. Gas delivery management systems regulate and monitor the flow of anesthetic gases, maintaining safety and effectiveness. Other equipment may include monitoring devices and recovery systems.</p></p>
