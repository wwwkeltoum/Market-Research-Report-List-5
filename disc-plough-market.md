<p><p>The disc plough market can be categorized into three main types: trail type, mounted type, and semi-mounted type. The trail type is designed to be towed by a tractor, offering high maneuverability and ease of use in various terrains. Mounted type ploughs are directly attached to the tractor, providing better control and stability, suitable for small to medium-sized farms. Semi-mounted types combine features of both, allowing for a balance of flexibility and efficiency in larger agricultural operations.</p></p>
