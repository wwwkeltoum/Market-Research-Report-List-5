<p><strong>The Portable Medical Vacuum System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Claw Pump Techology</li><li>Dry Rotary Vane Technology</li><li>Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Medical Vacuum System market features three main technologies: Dry Claw Pump Technology, which utilizes two claw-shaped rotors to create a vacuum without oil, ensuring a clean and maintenance-free operation; Dry Rotary Vane Technology, employing rotating vanes that operate without lubrication, providing efficient suction while minimizing contamination; and Oil-Sealed Rotary Vane Technology, which uses oil to lubricate and seal the vanes, delivering robust performance but requiring regular maintenance for optimal operation. Each technology caters to specific medical applications and cleanliness requirements.</p></p>
