<p><p>The packaging laminator market is categorized into two main types: semi-automatic and fully automatic laminators. Semi-automatic laminators require manual intervention for certain processes, making them suitable for small to medium production runs where flexibility is essential. In contrast, fully automatic laminators operate independently, streamlining the entire packaging process and enhancing efficiency for high-volume production environments. Both types serve different market needs, balancing cost, speed, and operational complexity.</p></p>
