<p><p>The IV Extension Set market is categorized into Single-Lumen (SL) and Double-Lumen (DL) types. Single-Lumen sets consist of one channel for fluid delivery, suitable for straightforward IV therapy. They are often utilized in less complex procedures. In contrast, Double-Lumen sets feature two separate channels, allowing for simultaneous infusion of multiple medications or fluids, which is beneficial in critical care settings. These distinctions cater to varying clinical needs, enhancing patient care and treatment flexibility.</p></p>
