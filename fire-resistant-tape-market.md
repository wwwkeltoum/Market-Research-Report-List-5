<p><p>The fire-resistant tape market comprises primarily two types: single-sided tape and double-sided tape. Single-sided tape features adhesive on one side, making it ideal for bonding or sealing applications where attachment to one surface is sufficient. In contrast, double-sided tape has adhesive on both sides, allowing it to join two surfaces securely, which enhances its utility in various insulation and construction projects. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and compliance in fire-prone environments, providing reliable performance under extreme conditions.</p></p>
