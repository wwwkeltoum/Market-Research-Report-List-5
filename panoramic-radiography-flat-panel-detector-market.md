<p><p>Panoramic radiography flat panel detectors are essential in dental imaging, employing various technologies. Silicon crystal detectors offer high sensitivity and resolution due to their crystalline structure, providing clear images with reduced radiation exposure. Amorphous silicon detectors, on the other hand, utilize non-crystalline silicon, allowing for flexible design and large-area coverage, making them suitable for capturing comprehensive panoramic images. Both types enhance diagnostic capabilities, with silicon crystal being more efficient in terms of image quality and amorphous silicon excelling in versatility.</p></p>
