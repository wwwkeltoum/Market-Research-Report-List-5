<p><strong>The Automobile Aluminium Alloy Plate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Rolling</li><li>Hot Rolling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile aluminium alloy plate market is categorized into cold rolling and hot rolling processes. Cold rolling involves applying pressure at room temperature, resulting in a smoother surface and increased strength, making it suitable for applications requiring precision and durability. In contrast, hot rolling takes place at elevated temperatures, allowing for easier shaping and forming of thicker plates. Each method offers distinct advantages; cold rolling is ideal for finished parts, while hot rolling is better for larger, structural components in automotive manufacturing.</p></p>
