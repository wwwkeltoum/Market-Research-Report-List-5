<p><p>The dry bulk liners market includes various types tailored for specific needs. **Standard Film Liners** are basic, cost-effective options for general cargo protection. **Conductive Film Liners** prevent static buildup, ideal for flammable or explosive materials. **Fluidising Liners** enhance flowability, reducing residue for powdered or granular goods. **Woven Liners** offer durability and tear resistance, suitable for heavy or abrasive materials. Each type addresses unique challenges in transporting dry bulk goods, ensuring safety, efficiency, and cargo integrity across industries.</p></p>
