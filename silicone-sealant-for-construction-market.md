<p><p>Silicone sealants for construction are primarily categorized into neutral cure and moisture cure types. Neutral cure sealants cure by releasing small molecules, making them ideal for sensitive materials like metal and glass, as they won't corrode. In contrast, moisture cure sealants rely on the presence of moisture for curing, making them suitable for outdoor applications where humidity is prevalent. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the specific requirements of a construction project, including adhesion, flexibility, and environmental resistance.</p></p>
