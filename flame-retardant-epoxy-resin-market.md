<p><p>The flame retardant epoxy resin market is primarily categorized into two types: reactive type and additive type. Reactive type epoxy resins incorporate flame retardant chemicals into their molecular structure, enhancing fire resistance during curing. In contrast, additive type epoxy resins involve blending flame retardant materials with existing resin formulations, providing flexibility in applications. Both types are essential for industries like aerospace, automotive, and construction, where fire safety is crucial, and they meet stringent regulatory requirements while offering varying performance characteristics.</p></p>
