The Picosecond Single Pulse type generates isolated pulses of light with durations in the picosecond range, ideal for precision applications like optical coherence tomography. In contrast, the Picosecond Pulse Train produces a series of ultra-short pulses at regular intervals, suitable for high-speed communication and material processing. Both types leverage advanced techniques for enhanced performance in various technological fields.</p></p>
