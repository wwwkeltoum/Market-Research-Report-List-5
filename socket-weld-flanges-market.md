<p><p>Socket weld flanges are categorized primarily into four types: Raised Face (RF), Male-Female (MFM), Tongue and Groove (TG), and Ring Joint (RJ). RF flanges feature a raised area for improved sealing, MFM flanges provide a precise fit through male and female designs, TG flanges incorporate a tongue and groove system for enhanced alignment and stability, while RJ flanges utilize a ring joint gasket for high-pressure applications. Each type serves specific connectivity and pressure needs in piping systems.</p></p>
