<p><p>The Continuous Peripheral Nerve Block Catheter market comprises three primary types: Stimulating Catheter, Non-Stimulating Catheter, and Over-The-Needle Catheter. Stimulating Catheters use electrical stimulation to identify nerve location, enhancing accuracy during placement. Non-Stimulating Catheters do not utilize electrical stimulation, relying on ultrasound guidance for placement. Over-The-Needle Catheters feature a design that allows the catheter to be placed through a needle, facilitating easy insertion and positioning. Each type serves specific clinical needs in regional anesthesia and pain management.</p></p>
