<p><ul><li>Series Type Hybrid Transmission System</li><li>Parallel Type Hybrid Transmission System</li><li>Mixed Type Hybrid Transmission System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive hybrid transmission systems are categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Series Type**: In this system, the engine powers a generator that supplies energy to an electric motor, allowing for efficient energy use and smooth acceleration.</p><p>2. **Parallel Type**: Here, both the combustion engine and electric motor can drive the wheels, providing flexibility and improved efficiency during various driving conditions.</p><p>3. **Mixed Type**: This combines features from both series and parallel systems, offering drivers the advantages of both energy sources for enhanced performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
