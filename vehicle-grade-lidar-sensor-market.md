<p><p>The Vehicle Grade LiDAR Sensor market comprises two main types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid-State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR utilizes rotating components to capture 3D environmental data, offering high precision and a wide field of view, but may be more prone to wear and tear. In contrast, Solid-State LiDAR employs fixed components for capturing data, enhancing durability and reliability while often being more cost-effective. Both technologies play vital roles in autonomous vehicles, enhancing navigation, obstacle detection, and overall safety.</p></p>
