<p><p>The vehicle security system market comprises several types, including alarms, immobilizers, remote keyless entry, passive keyless entry, and central locking systems. Alarms deter theft through sound alerts. Immobilizers prevent unauthorized engine start. Remote keyless entry allows drivers to lock/unlock vehicles from a distance, enhancing convenience. Passive keyless entry enables automatic locking/unlocking as the driver approaches or leaves, providing seamless access. Central locking systems synchronize all doors, improving overall security and ease of use for vehicle owners.</p></p>
