<p><p>The aerospace industry employs various crack detection methods to ensure structural integrity. Eddy Current testing utilizes electromagnetic induction to detect surface cracks in conductive materials. Dielectric tests assess materials by measuring electrical properties, identifying hidden defects. Imaging techniques, like X-ray and ultrasonic imaging, visualize internal cracks or flaws. Phased Array technology enhances ultrasonic testing by using multiple transducers to create detailed sectional images, improving the accuracy of crack detection in complex geometries. Together, these methods enhance safety and reliability in aerospace components.</p></p>
