<p><strong>The Hydro Turbine Generator Unit Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impulse Turbines</li><li>Reaction Turbines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydro turbine generator unit market primarily includes impulse and reaction turbines, which are essential for converting hydraulic energy into electrical power. Impulse turbines rely on high-velocity water jets to drive the runner, making them suitable for high-head applications. In contrast, reaction turbines operate by utilizing water pressure and buoyancy within the turbine, making them effective for lower head sites. Both types play crucial roles in renewable energy generation, contributing to sustainable electricity production and supporting global energy transitions.</p></p>
