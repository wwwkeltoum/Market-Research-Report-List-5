<p><p>The farm tractor seats market primarily consists of two types: mechanical suspension seats and air suspension seats. Mechanical suspension seats utilize springs and shock-absorbing materials to provide comfort and reduce vibrations during operation, making them a cost-effective option for many users. In contrast, air suspension seats use air-filled bladders to adjust support dynamically based on the operator's weight and movement, offering a higher level of comfort and customization. Both options are designed to enhance operator productivity and reduce fatigue.</p></p>
