<p><p>The anti-block additives market is primarily divided into two types: inorganic and organic. Organic anti-block additives, like polysiloxanes and polyethylenes, provide smoother surfaces and better processing capabilities. Both types are essential in industries like packaging, film production, and coatings, enabling improved product performance and functionality by reducing unwanted adhesion.</p></p>
