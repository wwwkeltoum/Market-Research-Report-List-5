<p><p>The bulk salt market is categorized into three main types: rock salt, solar salt, and evaporated salt. Rock salt is mined directly from underground deposits and is often used for de-icing roads. Solar salt is produced by evaporating seawater or brine in large ponds, resulting in natural sea salt, commonly used in food and industrial applications. Evaporated salt, created by heating brine to remove water, features a fine texture and high purity, making it ideal for table and industrial uses.</p></p>
