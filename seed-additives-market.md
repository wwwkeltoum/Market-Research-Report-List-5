<p><p>Seed additives in the market are primarily categorized into two types: dry form and liquid form. Dry form additives typically include powders and granules, often used for seed treatment to enhance germination and protect against pests and diseases. Liquid form additives, on the other hand, are solutions that can be sprayed or drenched on seeds, providing nutrients and bioactive compounds for improved growth. Each type offers unique advantages in application efficiency, storage, and effectiveness in promoting seed health.</p></p>
