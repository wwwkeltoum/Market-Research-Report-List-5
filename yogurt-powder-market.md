<p><p>The yogurt powder market is categorized into three main types: skimmed yogurt powder, semi-skimmed yogurt powder, and whole yogurt powder. Skimmed yogurt powder is produced by removing most of the fat, offering a lower-calorie option rich in protein. Semi-skimmed yogurt powder retains some fat, balancing taste and nutritional benefits. Whole yogurt powder contains all the fat, providing a creamier texture and richer flavor, making it ideal for culinary applications. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and dietary needs.</p></p>
