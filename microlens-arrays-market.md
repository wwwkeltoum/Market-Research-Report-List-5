<p><ul><li>Aspherical Microlens Array</li><li>Spherical Microlens Array</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microlens arrays market comprises primarily two types: aspherical microlens arrays and spherical microlens arrays. Aspherical microlens arrays feature a nonlinear surface profile that enhances optical performance by reducing aberrations and improving light capture efficiency, making them suitable for advanced applications like imaging systems and sensors. In contrast, spherical microlens arrays have a uniform curvature, offering simplicity in manufacturing and cost-effectiveness, often used in standard optical applications such as fiber optics and display technologies. Both types serve diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
