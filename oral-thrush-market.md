<p><strong>The Oral Thrush Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pseudomembranous</li><li>Erythematous</li><li>Hyperplastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oral thrush, caused by Candida overgrowth, presents primarily in three forms. Pseudomembranous thrush features creamy white lesions that can be wiped off, leaving a reddened surface. Erythematous thrush is characterized by red, inflamed areas, often without plaques, leading to soreness and discomfort. Hyperplastic thrush appears as extensive, white patches that cannot be easily removed and may harbor underlying tissue changes. Each type reflects different underlying conditions and severity, influencing treatment approaches in oral health management.</p></p>
