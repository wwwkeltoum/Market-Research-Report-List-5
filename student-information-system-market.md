<p><p>The Student Information System (SIS) market comprises two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based SIS offers flexibility, scalability, and remote access, allowing educational institutions to manage student data via the internet. This type reduces the need for extensive IT infrastructure and maintenance. In contrast, on-premise SIS requires local installation and management, providing institutions greater control over their data and security. However, it demands higher upfront costs and ongoing technical support, limiting scalability compared to cloud solutions.</p></p>
