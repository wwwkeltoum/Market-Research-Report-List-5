<p><p>The fruit and vegetable enzyme market includes several key types: pectinase, amylase, protease, and cellulase. Pectinase is used to break down pectin in fruits, enhancing juice extraction and clarity. Amylase converts starches into sugars, improving fermentation and sweetening processes. Protease aids in protein breakdown, enhancing texture and digestibility in food products. Cellulase targets cellulose in plant materials, facilitating fiber digestion and improving juice yield. These enzymes are crucial in food processing, biotechnology, and enhancing product quality.</p></p>
