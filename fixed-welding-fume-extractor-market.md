<p><p>The fixed welding fume extractor market comprises two primary types: stationary and portable models. Stationary extractors are permanently installed systems designed for high-capacity extraction, ideal for large industrial facilities with continuous welding operations. They provide efficient fume removal and are integrated into the workshop's ventilation system. In contrast, portable extractors are mobile units that allow flexibility and convenience for smaller workshops or on-site projects. They can be easily moved to different workstations, catering to various welding tasks and environments.</p></p>
