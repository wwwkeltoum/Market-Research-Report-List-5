<p><p>Account-Based Marketing (ABM) tools are categorized into cloud-based and on-premises markets. In contrast, on-premises ABM tools are installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant IT resources and infrastructure. Each market type serves distinct business needs depending on size, technology strategy, and marketing goals.</p></p>
