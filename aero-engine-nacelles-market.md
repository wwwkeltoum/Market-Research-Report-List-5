<p><ul><li>Wing Hang</li><li>Tail Hang</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aero engine nacelles market is categorized into wing-hang and tail-hang types based on their mounting position on the aircraft. Wing-hang nacelles, typically positioned under the wings, are favored for their aerodynamic efficiency and ease of maintenance, making them popular in commercial aviation. In contrast, tail-hang nacelles are installed at the rear of the aircraft, predominantly in business jets and some military aircraft. This design enhances noise reduction and reduces drag, catering to specialized aviation needs.</p></p>
