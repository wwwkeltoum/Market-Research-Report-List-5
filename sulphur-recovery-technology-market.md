<p><p>The Sulphur Recovery Technology Market comprises primarily two types: the Claus Process and Tail Gas Treatment. The Claus Process is the most prevalent method, converting hydrogen sulfide into elemental sulfur, effectively recovering valuable sulfur from gas streams. Tail Gas Treatment complements the Claus Process by further treating the byproducts to minimize emissions, ensuring higher sulfur recovery rates and compliance with environmental regulations. Together, they provide efficient solutions for managing sulfur emissions in various industries, particularly oil and gas.</p></p>
