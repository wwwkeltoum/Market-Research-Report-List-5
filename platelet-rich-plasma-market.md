<p><p>The Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) market consists of three main types: P-PRP, L-PRP, and L-PRF. P-PRP is characterized by a higher platelet concentration and is often used for orthopedic and dermatological applications. L-PRP contains fewer platelets and is typically used in aesthetic procedures due to its gentler nature. L-PRF, or Platelet Rich Fibrin, offers a scaffold for tissue regeneration and is favored in surgical applications. Each type serves distinct medical and cosmetic purposes, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
