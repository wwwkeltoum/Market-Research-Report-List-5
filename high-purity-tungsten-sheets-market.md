<p><p>The High Purity Tungsten Sheets market can be categorized into two main types: Alloy and Pure Metal. Alloy tungsten sheets are composed of tungsten combined with one or more other metals, enhancing properties like strength and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for specialized applications. In contrast, pure tungsten sheets consist of 99.9% tungsten, offering superior thermal and electrical conductivity, ideal for high-temperature and vacuum environments. Both types cater to diverse industries, including aerospace, electronics, and medical sectors.</p></p>
