<p><p>The AG glass market encompasses various types, including chemical etching glass and spraying glass. Chemical etching glass involves using acidic solutions to create intricate patterns and designs, enhancing both aesthetics and functionality. This type is often used in architectural applications. Spraying glass, on the other hand, involves applying a fine mist of paint to the surface, producing a smooth, uniform finish. Both methods are popular in interior design, architectural features, and decorative applications, offering unique visual effects and light diffusion.</p></p>
