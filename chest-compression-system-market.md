<p><p>The Chest Compression System market consists of two primary types: mechanical and electric systems. Mechanical systems utilize devices that apply consistent manual-like compressions to a patient's chest, ensuring effective blood circulation during cardiac emergencies. In contrast, electric systems leverage powered mechanisms to deliver precise compressions at regulated rates and depths, often incorporating advanced features for monitoring and feedback. Both types enhance the effectiveness of cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) while reducing the physical strain on medical personnel, ultimately improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
