<p><strong>The Drop On Demand (DOD) Inkjet Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal DOD</li><li>Piezoelectric DOD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Drop On Demand (DOD) inkjet inks market is primarily segmented into two types: Thermal DOD and Piezoelectric DOD. Thermal DOD technology uses heat to create ink droplets, making it suitable for high-speed printing applications, often seen in consumer printers. On the other hand, Piezoelectric DOD utilizes mechanical stress to eject ink droplets, offering greater control over droplet size and placement, which is advantageous for industrial and specialized printing processes. Both types cater to diverse printing needs across various sectors.</p></p>
