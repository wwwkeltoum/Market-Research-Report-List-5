<p><p>The high-performance sputtering target market is segmented into two main types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat and commonly used in various thin-film deposition applications, offering uniform coating and efficiency for large substrates. In contrast, rotating targets are cylindrical and designed to continuously rotate during the sputtering process, enhancing material utilization and reducing heating effects. Both target types play a crucial role in industries like electronics, optics, and solar energy, catering to diverse deposition requirements.</p></p>
