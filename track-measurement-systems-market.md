<p><p>Track measurement systems encompass various tools used to assess railway track conditions, primarily including track trolleys and track inspection vehicles. Track trolleys are lightweight, man-operated or motorized units that travel along the tracks to conduct manual measurements of gauge, alignment, and surface irregularities. In contrast, track inspection vehicles are more advanced, equipped with sophisticated technology for automated data collection and analysis, enabling comprehensive assessments of track health over large distances. Both types are essential for maintaining railway safety and efficiency.</p></p>
