<p><strong>The Semi-hermetic Screw Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-screw Compressors</li><li>Twin-screw Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-hermetic screw compressor market comprises two main types: single-screw and twin-screw compressors. Single-screw compressors utilize a single helical rotor for gas compression, offering simplicity and compact design, making them ideal for specific applications. In contrast, twin-screw compressors employ two interlocking rotors that efficiently compress refrigerants and gases, providing higher efficiency and versatility. These compressors are widely used in industrial refrigeration, HVAC systems, and process cooling, catering to diverse industry needs with varying performance requirements.</p></p>
