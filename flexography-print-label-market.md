<p><p>The flexography print label market encompasses various label types catering to diverse applications. Pressure-sensitive labels are self-adhesive and versatile, suitable for quick application. Glue-applied labels require adhesive application for attachment, often seen on bottles and jars. Heat-shrink labels conform to the shape of containers when heated, enhancing product visibility. Stretch sleeve labels feature elastic properties that wrap around products tightly, ideal for irregular shapes. In-mold labels are integrated during the molding process, providing durable branding directly on packaging.</p></p>
