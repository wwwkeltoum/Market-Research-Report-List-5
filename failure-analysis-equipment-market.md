<p><p>Failure Analysis Equipment encompasses various technologies used to investigate and diagnose defects in materials and components. Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM) provide detailed surface imaging, while Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEM) offer insights into internal structures at atomic resolution. Focused Ion Beam (FIB) systems enable precise material removal and analysis. Dual-beam systems combine FIB and SEM capabilities for enhanced functionality, allowing for simultaneous imaging and milling. Each type plays a crucial role in identifying failure mechanisms in semiconductors and other materials.</p></p>
