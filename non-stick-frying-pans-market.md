<p><p>The non-stick frying pans market is primarily divided into two types: PTFE-based and ceramic-based non-stick pans. PTFE-based non-stick pans utilize polytetrafluoroethylene to create a smooth, easy-to-clean surface, offering excellent heat conduction and durability. In contrast, ceramic-based non-stick pans feature a ceramic coating that is free from harmful chemicals, providing an eco-friendly alternative. While PTFE pans excel in non-stick performance, ceramic pans are increasingly preferred for their health-conscious attributes and aesthetic appeal, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
