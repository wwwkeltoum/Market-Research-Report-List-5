<p><p>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) system market comprises Automatic CPAP and Non-Automatic CPAP devices. Automatic CPAP systems intelligently adjust airway pressure throughout the night based on real-time breathing patterns, providing personalized therapy for patients with sleep apnea. In contrast, Non-Automatic CPAP devices maintain a fixed pressure setting, requiring users to adjust to their prescribed level without adaptive changes. Both types cater to specific patient needs, offering varying levels of comfort and effectiveness in managing obstructive sleep apnea.</p></p>
