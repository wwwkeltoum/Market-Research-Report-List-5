<p><strong>The Packaged Explosive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Dynamite</li><li>Packaged Emulsions & Water Gel</li><li>Packaged ANFO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The packaged explosive market comprises three main types: Traditional Dynamite, Packaged Emulsions & Water Gel, and Packaged ANFO. Traditional Dynamite consists of nitroglycerin-based explosives that offer high sensitivity and energy. Packaged Emulsions & Water Gel are water-resistant, safer alternatives featuring a blend of ammonium nitrate and other additives, making them ideal for wet conditions. Packaged ANFO (Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil) is a cost-effective, bulk solution combining ammonium nitrate and fuel oil, widely used in mining and construction due to its stability and performance.</p></p>
