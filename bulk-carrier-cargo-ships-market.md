<p><strong>The Bulk Carrier Cargo Ships Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Geared bulk carriers</li><li>Combined carriers</li><li>Gearless carriers</li><li>Self-dischargers</li><li>Lakers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bulk carrier cargo ships are categorized based on their loading and unloading capabilities. Geared bulk carriers have onboard cranes for self-loading and discharging, while gearless carriers rely on port facilities for cargo handling. Combined carriers can transport both dry bulk and liquid cargo, offering versatility. Self-dischargers utilize conveyor systems to efficiently unload cargo without port equipment. Lakers are specifically designed for operation in the Great Lakes, catering to regional demands with size limitations suited for inland waterways.</p></p>
