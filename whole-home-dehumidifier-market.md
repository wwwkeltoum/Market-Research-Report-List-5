<p><ul><li>Ducted Dehumidifier</li><li>Free Standing Dehumidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The whole-home dehumidifier market comprises two main types: ducted dehumidifiers and free-standing dehumidifiers. Ducted dehumidifiers integrate into existing HVAC systems, effectively managing humidity levels across multiple rooms through ductwork. They offer a centralized solution and are ideal for larger homes. In contrast, free-standing dehumidifiers operate independently and are portable, allowing users to place them in specific areas where humidity is a concern. They are generally easier to install and suitable for targeted dehumidification needs.</p></p>
