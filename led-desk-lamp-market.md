<p><p>The LED desk lamp market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless. Wired LED desk lamps connect directly to a power source, offering consistent brightness and eliminating the need for battery replacements. They are often favored for their reliability and often lower cost. Wireless LED desk lamps, on the other hand, operate on rechargeable batteries, providing flexibility and portability. These lamps are ideal for spaces without easy access to power outlets, catering to consumers who prioritize convenience and mobility.</p></p>
