<p><ul><li>Bituminous</li><li>Elastomeric</li><li>Cementitious</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid Applied Membrane (LAM) for construction comprises three main types: Bituminous, Elastomeric, and Cementitious. </p><p>Bituminous membranes are polymer-modified asphalt solutions, noted for their waterproofing and durability, commonly used on roofs. Elastomeric membranes are flexible, rubber-like materials that offer excellent elongation and resilience against cracking, making them suitable for various surfaces. Cementitious membranes consist of a cement-based composition, providing excellent adhesion and durability, primarily used in areas requiring protective waterproofing, such as basements and water tanks. Each type serves specific environmental and structural needs in the construction industry.</p></p>
