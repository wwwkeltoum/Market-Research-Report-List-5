<p><strong>The MRI Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open MRI systems</li><li>Closed MRI systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI systems market is primarily divided into two types: open MRI systems and closed MRI systems. Open MRI systems feature a wide bore design, providing a more comfortable experience for patients with claustrophobia or larger body types. However, they may offer lower resolution images. In contrast, closed MRI systems have a tighter, cylindrical shape that delivers higher resolution imaging, making them the preferred choice for detailed diagnostics. Each type serves distinct patient needs and imaging requirements in the healthcare market.</p></p>
