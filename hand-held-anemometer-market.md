<p><p>The hand-held anemometer market comprises several types, each suited for different applications. Cup anemometers measure wind speed using rotating cups. Vane anemometers utilize a propeller to gauge airflow direction and velocity. Hot-wire anemometers measure flow by detecting changes in temperature. Laser Doppler anemometers employ laser techniques for precise velocity measurements. Ultrasonic anemometers use sound waves to determine wind speed and direction. Additionally, there are other specialized types serving unique measurement needs in various fields.</p></p>
