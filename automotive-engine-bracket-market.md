<p><ul><li>Passive Elastomeric Brackets</li><li>Active Engine Bracket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engine bracket market comprises two primary types: passive elastomeric brackets and active engine brackets. Passive elastomeric brackets are designed to absorb vibrations and noise, using rubber or elastomer materials to enhance comfort and stability in vehicles. In contrast, active engine brackets employ advanced technologies, such as actuators and sensors, to dynamically adjust and counteract engine vibrations in real-time, providing superior noise and vibration control. Both types play crucial roles in improving vehicle performance and passenger comfort.</p></p>
