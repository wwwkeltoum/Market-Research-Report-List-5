<p><ul><li>Bituminous</li><li>Elastomeric</li><li>Cementitious</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Liquid Applied Waterproofing Membranes market encompasses three primary types: Bituminous, Elastomeric, and Cementitious. Bituminous membranes, made from asphalt or coal tar, offer excellent water resistance and durability, making them suitable for various applications. Elastomeric membranes provide flexibility and elongation, ideal for areas subject to movement or temperature fluctuations. Cementitious membranes, composed of cement-based materials, offer a strong, rigid waterproof layer, often utilized in basements and other static structures. Each type serves distinct needs in waterproofing applications.</p></p>
