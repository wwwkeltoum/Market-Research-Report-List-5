<p><p>The PLC fiber splitter market encompasses various types based on the number of output ports, including 1x4, 1x8, 1x16, and others. A 1x4 splitter divides one input fiber into four outputs, ideal for smaller networks, while 1x8 splits into eight outputs, suitable for medium-sized applications. The 1x16 model extends this to sixteen outputs, catering to larger networks. Other configurations may include higher port counts or specialized designs, allowing for flexible deployment in diverse telecommunication and data transmission scenarios.</p></p>
