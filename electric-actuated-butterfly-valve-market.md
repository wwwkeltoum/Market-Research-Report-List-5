<p><p>The electric actuated butterfly valve market features two primary types: rubber sealed and metal sealed valves. Rubber sealed valves utilize elastomeric materials to create a tight seal, making them ideal for applications with lower pressure and non-corrosive fluids. In contrast, metal sealed valves employ precision-ground metal surfaces for sealing, offering enhanced durability and resistance to high temperatures and aggressive substances, thus suitable for demanding industrial environments. Both types serve critical roles across various sectors, including water treatment and chemical processing.</p></p>
