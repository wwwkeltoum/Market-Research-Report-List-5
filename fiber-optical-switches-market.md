<p><ul><li>Single-mode Fiber Optical Switches</li><li>Multimode Fiber Optical Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optical switches market is primarily segmented into single-mode and multimode fiber optical switches. Single-mode fiber optical switches are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single light path to reduce signal loss and allow high-speed data transmission. In contrast, multimode fiber optical switches cater to shorter distances, employing multiple light paths to transmit data quickly but with slightly higher signal attenuation. Both types are essential for enhancing communication networks, supporting applications in telecommunications, data centers, and enterprise environments.</p></p>
