<p><strong>The Super-Resolution Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>STED</li><li>PALM</li><li>STORM</li><li>SIM</li><li>FPALM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Super-Resolution Imaging encompasses advanced microscopy techniques that surpass the diffraction limit of light. Key types include STED (Stimulated Emission Depletion), which uses fluorophore saturation to achieve high resolution; PALM (Photo-Activated Localization Microscopy) and STORM (Stochastic Optical Reconstruction Microscopy), both relying on single-molecule imaging for precise spatial resolution; SIM (Structured Illumination Microscopy), which enhances contrast using patterned illumination; and FPALM (Fluorescence Photo-Activation Localization Microscopy), a variation that combines features of PALM and fluorescence techniques for improved imaging in biological studies.</p></p>
