<p><p>Cast stretch films are categorized into several types based on the polymer used. Polyethylene (PE) films are the most common due to their excellent stretchability and durability, making them ideal for pallet wrapping. Polypropylene (PP) films offer high clarity and puncture resistance but are less stretchable than PE. Polyamide (PA) films are known for their strong barrier properties and are suitable for packaging products requiring extended shelf life. Other films may include blends or engineered materials designed for specific applications, enhancing functionality and performance.</p></p>
