The market for RARs includes several types: </p><p>1. **Single Mode Type**: These are optimized for a single wavelength, offering high precision in applications like telecommunication.</p><p>2. **Polarization Maintaining Type**: Designed to retain the polarization of light, they're essential in applications requiring consistent polarization characteristics.</p><p>3. **Multimode Type**: These can handle multiple wavelengths, making them versatile for various lighting and sensing applications, though with less precision than single-mode types.</p></p>
