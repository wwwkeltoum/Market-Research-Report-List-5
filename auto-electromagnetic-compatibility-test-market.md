<p><strong>The Auto Electromagnetic Compatibility Test Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bulk Current Injection (BCI)</li><li>Conducted Emissions (CE)</li><li>Conducted Transient Emissions (CTE)</li><li>Conducted Transient Immunity (CTI)</li><li>Electrostatic Discharge (ESD)</li><li>Radiated Emissions (RE)</li><li>Radiated Immunity (RI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auto Electromagnetic Compatibility Test Market encompasses various testing types essential for evaluating the electromagnetic performance of automotive components. Bulk Current Injection (BCI) measures immunity to conducted disturbances. Conducted Emissions (CE) assesses unwanted emissions in power and signal lines, while Conducted Transient Emissions (CTE) evaluates transient disturbances. Conducted Transient Immunity (CTI) tests resistance to transient events. Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) tests susceptibility to static electricity. Radiated Emissions (RE) and Radiated Immunity (RI) examine emissions into space and immunity to external radiated fields, respectively.</p></p>
