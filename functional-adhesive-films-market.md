<p><p>The Functional Adhesive Films market primarily consists of three types: Hot Melt Adhesive Films, Pressure Sensitive Adhesive Films, and Others. Hot Melt Adhesive Films are thermoplastic polymers that bond when heated, offering strong adhesion and quick setting times ideal for packaging and automotive applications. Pressure Sensitive Adhesive Films bond upon contact, making them versatile for labels and tapes. The "Others" category includes specialized films like conductive or thermally conductive adhesives, catering to niche applications in electronics and medical sectors.</p></p>
