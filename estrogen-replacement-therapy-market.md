<p><strong>The Estrogen Replacement Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oral</li><li>Topical</li><li>Transdermal</li><li>Vaginal</li><li>Parenteral</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Estrogen Replacement Therapy (ERT) is available in various forms to cater to individual needs. Oral ERT involves pills taken daily, while topical forms, such as gels or creams, are applied directly to the skin. Transdermal patches release estrogen gradually through the skin. Vaginal ERT, including creams or rings, targets localized symptoms, providing direct relief. Parenteral ERT encompasses injections, offering a more immediate effect. Each type has its advantages depending on patient preference, absorption rates, and specific health considerations.</p></p>
