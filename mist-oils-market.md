<p><p>Mist oils are specialized lubricants used in various industrial applications, categorized primarily into two types: non-strain and anti-wear. Non-strain type mist oils are designed for general lubrication without significant pressure, providing consistent performance in light-duty machinery. In contrast, anti-wear mist oils are formulated to protect against wear and tear in higher-stress environments, incorporating additives that enhance surface protection and longevity of components. Both types are essential for optimizing equipment efficiency and reducing maintenance costs in industrial settings.</p></p>
