<p><p>Automotive catalytic converters are emission control devices that reduce harmful pollutants from vehicle exhaust. **Two-way converters** primarily oxidize carbon monoxide (CO) and unburned hydrocarbons (HC) into less harmful gases like carbon dioxide (CO2) and water (H2O). **Three-way converters** additionally reduce nitrogen oxides (NOx) into nitrogen (N2) and oxygen (O2), making them more efficient for gasoline engines. **Other types** include diesel oxidation catalysts and selective catalytic reduction systems, tailored for specific emissions and fuel types, enhancing environmental compliance across diverse automotive applications.</p></p>
