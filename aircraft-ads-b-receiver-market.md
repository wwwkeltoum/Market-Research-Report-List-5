<p><p>The Aircraft ADS-B (Automatic Dependent Surveillance–Broadcast) receiver market can be categorized into three types: receivers, transceivers, and transmitters. Receivers are devices that capture ADS-B signals from other aircraft, enhancing situational awareness and safety. Transceivers serve dual functions, acting as both receivers and transmitters, allowing aircraft to send their own data while receiving information from others. Transmitters solely broadcast an aircraft's position and information. Together, these components play crucial roles in modern air traffic management and surveillance systems.</p></p>
