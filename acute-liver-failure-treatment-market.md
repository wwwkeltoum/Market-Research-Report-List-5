Assisted liver transplantation combines traditional donor organs with support devices to bridge patients until a suitable donor is found. Xenotransplantation investigates the use of animal organs, particularly porcine, to address the shortage of human donor organs. Each method aims to improve survival rates and liver function recovery in patients facing acute liver failure.</p></p>
