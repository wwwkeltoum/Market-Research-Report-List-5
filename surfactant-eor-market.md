<p><p>The Surfactant Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) market is primarily segmented into two types: Anionic Surfactants and Other Surfactants. Anionic surfactants, which carry a negative charge, are effective in reducing interfacial tension between oil and water, improving oil mobility. Other surfactants include cationic, nonionic, and amphoteric types, each with distinct properties that can enhance oil recovery under specific conditions. These surfactants optimize oil displacement and improve overall recovery efficiency in reservoirs, catering to varying geological and operational scenarios.</p></p>
