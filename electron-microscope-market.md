<p><strong>The Electron Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electron Microscopy (SEM)</li><li>Transmission Electron Microscopy (TEM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electron microscope market comprises two primary types: Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM) and Transmission Electron Microscopy (TEM). SEM provides detailed three-dimensional images of surfaces by scanning with a focused beam of electrons, ideal for material science and biology. In contrast, TEM allows for high-resolution imaging of thin samples by transmitting electrons through them, making it essential for examining internal structures at the atomic level. Both techniques are critical in research and industrial applications, driving advancements in various fields.</p></p>
