<p><p>The Inline Automated X-ray Inspection (AXI) market primarily consists of two types: 2D AXI and 3D AXI. 2D AXI offers flat image diagnostics, ideal for detecting surface defects in solder joints and component placements, providing rapid inspection in high-volume production. In contrast, 3D AXI leverages advanced imaging techniques to evaluate complex assemblies, offering deeper insights into internal structures and potential hidden defects. Both technologies enhance quality assurance and streamline manufacturing processes within the electronics industry.</p></p>
