<p><ul><li>One-Component Acrylic Filler</li><li>Two-Component Acrylic Filler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic filler market is segmented into two primary types: one-component and two-component acrylic fillers. One-component acrylic fillers are ready-to-use products that require no mixing and are ideal for quick applications, making them popular for DIY projects and smaller repair jobs. In contrast, two-component acrylic fillers require mixing a resin and a hardener, providing superior adhesion and durability, making them suitable for industrial applications and heavy-duty repairs. These distinctions guide consumer choice based on application needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
