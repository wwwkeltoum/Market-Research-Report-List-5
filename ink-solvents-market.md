<p><p>The ink solvents market is categorized into various types based on their chemical composition and applications. Alcohol solvents, such as ethanol and isopropanol, are widely used for fast-drying inks. Ester solvents, known for their excellent solvency and low toxicity, enhance ink performance and adhesion. Benzene solvents, although effective, face regulatory scrutiny due to health concerns. Ketone solvents, like acetonitrile, offer rapid evaporation rates and good solvency, making them suitable for specific ink formulations, particularly in industrial applications.</p></p>
