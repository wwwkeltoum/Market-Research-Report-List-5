<p><p>The Powder Caramel Color market is categorized into three main types: Plain Caramel Color, Ammonia Caramel Color, and Ammonia Sulfite Caramel Color. Plain Caramel Color is derived from simple heating of sugars and is commonly used in food and beverages for coloring. Ammonia Caramel Color uses ammonia in its production, resulting in a darker hue and stronger flavor, often utilized in sauces and dark beers. Ammonia Sulfite Caramel Color incorporates sulfites, offering stability and a unique color profile, favored in various processed foods.</p></p>
