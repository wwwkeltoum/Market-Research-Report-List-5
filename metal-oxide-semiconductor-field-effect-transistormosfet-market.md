Low-pressure MOSFETs operate at reduced atmospheric pressures, enhancing performance in applications requiring high efficiency and low power consumption. In contrast, medium-pressure MOSFETs function within a moderate pressure range, providing a balance between performance and thermal management. Both types are essential in various electronic applications, including power management, automotive systems, and consumer electronics, driving advances in technology and efficiency.</p></p>
