<p><ul><li>Wave-induced Motion SUBs</li><li>Stirred SUBs</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-use bioreactors market is categorized into three main types: wave-induced motion single-use bioreactors (SUBs), stirred SUBs, and others. Wave-induced motion SUBs utilize gentle agitation through wave-like motions, promoting cell growth and homogeneity without mechanical parts. Stirred SUBs employ mechanical stirring for consistent mixing and oxygen transfer, ideal for larger scales. The "others" category encompasses alternative designs and emerging technologies, addressing specific industry needs such as scalability, cost-effectiveness, and reduced contamination risks in bioprocessing applications.</p></p>
