<p><p>Multistage centrifugal blowers are categorized into two main types: oil-free and oil-immersed. Oil-free blowers operate without lubrication, ensuring clean and contamination-free air, making them ideal for applications in industries such as food processing and pharmaceuticals. In contrast, oil-immersed blowers use lubricating oil for smooth operation and efficient cooling, which can enhance performance but may introduce the risk of oil contamination. Each type serves distinct industry needs, balancing efficiency, maintenance, and air purity requirements.</p></p>
