<p><p>The vanadate laser crystal market primarily includes three types: Nd:YVO4, Nd:GdVO4, and Nd:LuVO4. Nd:YVO4 is widely used for solid-state lasers due to its high efficiency and excellent thermal conductivity. Nd:GdVO4 offers higher gain and can operate at lower pump powers, making it suitable for portable applications. Nd:LuVO4, known for its high thermal stability and efficiency, is ideal for high-power laser systems. Each crystal type caters to specific industrial, medical, and scientific applications within the laser technology field.</p></p>
